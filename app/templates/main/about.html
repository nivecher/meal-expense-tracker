About Meal Expense Tracker

What is Meal Expense Tracker?

A modern web application that helps you track dining expenses, analyze spending patterns, and maintain a history of your culinary experiences. Built with Flask and designed for food enthusiasts who want to understand their dining habits and manage their meal budgets effectively.

Perfect for: Food bloggers, budget-conscious diners, business expense tracking, culinary adventurers, and anyone who wants to gain insights into their dining patterns.
